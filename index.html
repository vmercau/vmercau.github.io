About

Hi! Based in Buenos Aires, Argentina, I work at the interface of biology and physics, using quantitative microscopy and computational analysis to study how cells respond and make decisions. My PhD research (CONICET scholarship) focuses on yeast cell morphology and Information Theory, combining experiments with data-driven modeling, and developing reproducible workflows in R/Python. I love teaching and enjoy helping students build intuition through clear explanations and problem-solving.
